Introduction to Drama and Dramatic Theory（例） - wolai 笔记
对戏剧简单的介绍
Aristotle and Poetics
简单介绍，反对柏拉图的理念说idea，三张桌子，概念→现实→摹仿的摹仿，在文艺方面与柏拉图的分歧
主要理论

亚里士多德的悲剧观

  1. Poetry is an art with its own Object
  2. Poetry includes epic poetry, tragedy, comedy, dithyrambic poetry酒神赞歌, and even some kinds of music戏剧的种类
  3. His general scheme of human action: Thought,action and production
  4. Poetry and the arts are included under the head ofimitation( mimesis), one of the divisions of production
  5. Literature is a rational and beneficial activity, notirrational and dangerous as it was for Plato
  6. The nature of Drama
    A performative art表演艺术
    The imitation (representation) of an action (Aristotle Poetics后来回归到日常

悲剧六要素

  • Plot
  • Character: appearance, speech, action, otherscomments
  • Thought: reasoning aspect (including theme), Provoking thought
  • Diction: language (mainly dialogue) clear, interesting, appropriate, dynamic (energetic)戏剧是表演的艺术
  • Music: sound effects, tonal pattern，古罗马，corals
  • Spectacle: visual aspects of theatric production (scenery, lighting, costume, make-up, the movement of the actors场景调度

Plot

  • Structure: the things that happen in the play and the ways in whichthose incidents connect
  • Plot VS story
    事件之间如何串联，就是plot
    Plot Mountain以哈姆雷特为例
    Expoition介绍
    Rising action发展，比如悬疑片，涉及到冲突。哈姆雷特和叔父的矛盾，辅以其与母亲的矛盾、与爱人的矛盾
    Climax哈姆雷特与雷欧提斯决斗
  • Episodic plot and involved plot
  • Conflict
  • Exposition
    • Foreshadowing麦克白的巫师
    • Surprise
    • Suspense悬念，哈姆雷特父亲托梦
  • Complications/the point of attack: the balance is disturbed for the first time
  • Rising action
  • Climax
  • Falling action denouement收场

Charter

First, the hero must be good, and thus manifest moral purpose in his speech
Second, the hero must have propriety, or many valor.
Thirdly, the hero must be 'true to life' And finally, the hero must be consistent.
莎士比亚每一个主人公都代表人性的某一个缺陷
理论发展，圆形人物

戏剧类型

Tragedy

Imitation of actions. Serious and important
Origin: Greek tragos, "song of the goat"(dionysus'ssacred animal), reflecting the Greeks 's view of the relationship between man and the gods
词源于希腊，神（酒神）与人
An assertion of the greatness of humanity, mans abilityto rise to the heights of human dignity I nthe face of anopposing torce
展现人性的伟大，You can destroy me, but never defeat me.
It deals with the inevitable, but is not saddening ordepressing, it's positive and optimistic
悲剧的内容
Purgative Catharsis净化 the arousal in the audience of pity and fear (pity for the tragic heros plight,and fear that his fate might befall us.)
净化作用，陶冶人的情操，悲剧的命运无法改变
The protagonist主人公 is a human being of a certain noble stature (originally nobility, today human greatness) with tragic flaw
人物
Irony
dramatic irony观众知道的东西，要比剧中人物所知道的要多

喜剧

Aristotle's comedy aims at representing men as worse in actual
增加观众对生活的认知
It focuses on the commonplace of everyday life, the follies, foibles and vices
喜剧悲剧关注点不同，喜剧更多关注日常生活中
Using distortion and exaggeration, stock characters, stock situations (mistaken identity or screen scene: a character is eavesdropping behind a screen)
exaggeration舞台上的夸张；stock character固定的角色，比如莎士比亚笔下的弄臣；stock situation王子与贫儿，人物替换，老生常谈的情节

Tragicomedy悲喜剧

Early concept: bits of comedy added onto atragedy, lacking both the awe associated with tragedy and the fun associated with comedy
现代戏剧得到大发展，比如荒诞派《等待戈多》
2021.02.26
The theatre of the absurd
Characterless, plotless, open ending

Unities in drama三一律

unity of action, unity of place, and unity of time
the three principles derived by French classicists from Aristotles Poetics: they require a play to have action represented as occurring in a single place within the course of a day
The unities were of much less concern in Renaisse, England. Christopher Marlowe, Wlliam Shakespear, Jonson often included two or more plots in a play, mixed comedy and tragedy, and freely switched
The prestige of the unities continued to dominate drama until the Romantic era, when it was destroy evening of catcalls and violence, with the opening Hugo' s Romantic tragedy Hernani《欧那尼》（1830）对法国影响很大，直到浪漫主义时期雨果《欧那尼》问世

Dramatic theory since Aristotle

浪漫主义

The romantics protested against French neoclassic and Aristotelian drama
The first important romantic critic G.E. Lessing莱辛《汉堡剧评》
"The misfortune of those whose circumstances most resemble our own, must naturally penetrate mosdeeply into our hearts".—damburg Dramaturgy
关注日常的生活，而非英雄，使用普通人的语言，而非华贵的辞藻
Structure dramatic action based on human experience and adopt gothic style
Anti-mimesis theatre反模仿说

19th century naturalism and realism

Emile Zola argues for a theatre ofscientific objectivity 左拉，对生活客观化的呈现
lbsen (1828-1906) 易卜生
Peer Gynt, A Dolls House, And Ghosts
Social problems: money womensposition《玩偶之家》，娜拉出走
Chekhov(1860-1904): The Seagull, Three Sisters, The Cherry Orchard
Follow the realistic tradition, describethe daily life of the common people

20th century

Early stage

  1. Social，George Bernard Shaw萧伯纳，讽刺，戏剧改变社会
  2. Metaphysical: anti-realistic
  3. 各种流派，各种实验，绘画、小说、戏剧相互借鉴
    1. Psychoanalysis: Freud Jung佛洛伊德
    2. Symbolism
    3. Futurism未来派: dynamism, speed, energy and the power of the machine
      波丘尼《空间连续的独特形体》
    4. Expressionism: subiective view of life, externalize the internal thought of the characters through dreaming, etc
  4. Brecht's Epic theatre
  5. Formal: The Prague布拉格 school and New Criticism形式批评，各媒介批评趋同，针对文本本身

Later stage

  1. The Theatre of the Absurd荒诞派，一套批评体系
  2. Semiotic analysis of drama based on Roland Barthes and Umberto ECO语义学分析，罗兰巴特
  3. Poststructuralism: the displacement, the disjuncture, the libidinal flows（意识流反理性）
    后结构主义，也被称为解构主义，没有放之四海皆准的rules，代表人物德里达，批判书面—口语、男—女等二元对立概念，以及二元背后的权力，包括福柯等人，也在研究权力如何在潜意识中运作
    结构主义尝试decode语言，对整个语言表达的建构，rules共性。分析神话的结构，小说的结构
  4. British Cultural materialism
  5. American New historicism
  6. Feminism
  7. Post-colonialism反殖民，从意识形态领域角度探究，比如英国作家笔下的印度
